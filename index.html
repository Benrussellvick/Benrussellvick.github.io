Ben Russell-Vick - Frontend Developer


I’m an experienced Creative web developer with 6 years digital industry experience, building a range of websites from small to large-scale as well as web-applications based on web standard technologies. I have delivered engaging and visually compelling web builds on a variety of Frontend frameworks with user-friendly UI that are mobile first, cross-browser compatible and follow best practice allowing for seamless additional future development. As a frontend developer in an ever changing industry I’m always willing to learn and take my skills to the next level. Enthusiastic and hard-working with a superb work ethic.

I graduated in 2013 from Bournemouth University with a 2:1 where I was taught a broad variety of different coding languages and techniques. Over my 3 years I had to demonstrate the capacity to navigate a complex evolving media landscape that both challenged and informed peoples understanding of digital media.

During my time in the industry I've continuously developed responsive, semantic websites using HTML5, JS and CSS. In certain companies I introduced new design systems to help streamline productivity, communication and collaboration within the creative process. Mentored junior developers allowing them to work alongside me and others to deliver web projects effectively. Developed rapid design prototypes iteratively generating feedback early and often in the process, improving the final design and reducing the need for future changes.
